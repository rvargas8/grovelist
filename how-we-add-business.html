How We Add Your Business

Getting listed on The Grove List is simple — and it's completely free. We list every legitimate local business in Southern Hillsborough County that asks to be here. No forms, no fees, no waiting weeks for approval.

How it works

Just reach out by email or WhatsApp with a few details about your business, and we'll have you listed within 24 hours. That's it.

We'll need:

  • Your business name
  • What you do (a sentence or two)
  • Your phone number or website (if you have one)
  • Your category (Restaurant, Retail, Service, Health, Sports, or Other)

What is a Grove Pick?

Grove Picks are businesses we've personally visited and would genuinely recommend to a neighbor. They're marked with a badge on the listing and featured at the top of the directory. Grove Picks are our honest opinion — not paid placements, not sponsored spots.

If you'd like to be considered for a Grove Pick, just let us know when you reach out. We'll be straightforward with you about whether it's a fit.

Can I update my listing?

Yes. If your hours, phone number, or description changes, just send us a message and we'll update it same day.
